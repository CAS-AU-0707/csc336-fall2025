Meet The Team

At Monsters, Inc. we may collect screams — but out goal is to power our world with creativity, teamwork, and monster-level dedication.

Before we introduce our incredible crew, we just want to say: this team is the real scare-force behind everything we do. Talented, driven, and always ready to roll (or roar), they make the magic happen every single day.

Now let’s meet the monsters who make it all possible.

👁 Mike Wazowski – Operations & Efficiency Manager 👁

Small in size but larger than life, Mike Wazowski is the unstoppable force behind the scenes. With his trusty clipboard and boundless energy, Mike makes sure everything runs like clockwork. He’s a master at schedules, logistics, and finding solutions faster than you can say “Monstropolis.” When he’s not cracking jokes or giving motivational pep talks, Mike can be found keeping Sulley on track (and reminding everyone who the real brains of the operation is). His humor, optimism, and one-liners light up the room—just like his big green eye.

Fun Fact: Mike insists he’s photogenic… as long as you don’t cover him up with the company logo again.

🐾 James P. Sullivan – Chief Executive Scare-gist 🐾

With a roar that once echoed through Monstropolis, Sulley has proven he’s not only the top scarer in the business—but also a natural leader with a heart as big as his fur. Towering and blue (with the perfect touch of purple spots), Sulley inspires confidence just by walking into a room.

These days, he’s traded in jump-scares for leadership skills, guiding the team with empathy, wisdom, and a knack for solving problems with both strength and kindness. He’s the perfect balance of powerhouse and gentle giant—making sure everyone feels safe, heard, and motivated.

Fun Fact: Sulley’s fur takes 231 brushes a day to keep it looking fluffy. Don’t ask how we know.

🧾 Roz – Compliance & Records Supervisor 🧾

No one slips through the cracks on Roz’s watch. With her sharp eye, monotone wit, and endless stack of paperwork, she keeps every file, form, and policy in perfect order. She may seem gruff at first, but Roz’s attention to detail is what ensures the entire operation runs smoothly—and legally. Roz is the master of accountability, reminding the team (in her unmistakable voice) that “I’m watching you… always watching.” Behind her glasses and no-nonsense attitude, though, is someone who truly cares about keeping things fair and square.

Fun Fact: Roz can alphabetize faster than a shredder—and she enjoys it.
