Core Rules (beginner version)

  • Serve: Underhand, below the waist, diagonally cross-court, behind the baseline.
  • Double bounce: Each side must let the ball bounce once after the serve before volleys.
  • Kitchen: No volleys while standing in the non-volley zone.
  • Scoring: Only the serving side scores. Games commonly go to 11 (win by 2).
  • Faults: Hitting out, into the net, volleying in the kitchen, or missing the serve box.
